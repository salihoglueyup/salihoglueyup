## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security issues seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please follow these steps:

1. **Do not** open a public issue.
2. Email us privately at **<eyupzekisalihoglu@gmail.com>**.
3. Provide meaningful details about how to reproduce the vulnerability.

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and work with you to resolve the issue.
